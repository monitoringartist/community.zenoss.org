    Zenoss-Testing Forum

    Loading...
    Zenoss-Testing is our forum for discussing issues related to current and future beta tests.  When posting regarding beta issues, please verify your defect is a regression from previous versions and if you open a ticket, please followup on your post and add the link. You may use the zenoss/zenoss account to create tickets or use your own account. If you don't have a Trac account and would like one, please contact Matt Ray and he will get one created for you.  Click on the 'Discussions' tab and then click 'Set as default tab' for the threaded view by default.
