    About ZenPack Development

    This is the Community site documenting ZenPack development. There is a zenoss-zenpacks forum dedicated to ZenPack discussion. A comprehensive ZenPack directory is available on the Zenoss Wiki. There is also a ZenPack Developers group for ZenPack authors.

    Developing your own ZenPacks

    The authoritative location for all ZenPack development documentation is the ZenPack Development section of the Extending Zenoss page on the Zenoss Wiki.
