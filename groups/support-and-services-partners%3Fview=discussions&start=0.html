Announcement: Digital Parallels to offer Zenoss Enterprise as a Managed Service
  • Show Details
  • Hide Details

Digital Parallels is a leading UK Managed Services Provider offering a unique range of services with a strong focus on customer satisfaction. Leading, innovative technologies coupled with a passion to deliver excellent customer services enables Digital Parallels to provide services at an attractive price point that are simple to setup and simple to operate. As Zenoss' Premier Partner in the United Kingdom, Digital Parallels works with other partners to provide quality managed solutions to both the SMB and Enterprise market.

 

As well as providing dedicated on site Zenoss Core and Zenoss Enterprise installations to their customer base, Digital Parallels provides a multi-tenant remote monitoring service based on Zenoss Enterprise.  Customers can benefit from the commercial support and feature set of Zenoss Enterprise without an upfront subscriptions fee and gain the additional benefit of having their monitoring fully managed. With a simple per month pricing model customers  'out-source' their monitoring to Digital Parallels, enabling them to focus on what they do best - running their business's.

 

The MSP model enables customers traditionally located within Co-location and Managed Hosting companies to expand their visibility within the hosted solution without infringing the compliance or the SLA's hosting companies offer. A single pane of glass provides a centralised view of their hosted assets regardless of hosting provider or geographic location enabling monitoring from different locations to be combined in one 'window'.
