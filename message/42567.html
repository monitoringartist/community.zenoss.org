    Re: Zenprocess, zenwin, zeneventlog down after 2.5 upgrade
    I tried to analyze and was able to fix this issue. Cause of the problem are already installed ZenPacks.


    For me the following ZenPacks were installed before the upgrade:

    • WindowsWMIDeviceTemplateV2
    • ZenPacks.HP.Proliant.MIBs
    • ZenPacks.community.HPMon
    • ZenPacks.community.deviceAdvDetail
    • ZenPacks.zenoss.DellMonitor
    • ZenPacks.zenoss.HPMonitor

    After removing these ZenPacks, all services are starting correctly again. Hope this helps everyone who has the same problem after upgrading from 2.4.5 to 2.5.
