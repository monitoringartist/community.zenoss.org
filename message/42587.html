    Jive doesn't explicitly support Opera, but we've passed your comments along to them and this was their response:

     

    While we don't officially support Opera, it does work in our next major release (4.0.x) because we updated the version of TinyMCE that drives the RTE.  In 3.0.x the version of TinyMCE does target Opera specifically to load differently (and it looks like it fails, I was able to reproduce).  I am not sure why the creators of TinyMCE targeted Opera with specific code, but there is probably a different bug that is exposed by identifying as Firefox.  But any RTE is better then no RTE.

    I have let our developer that works with the RTE know, maybe he can slip in a quick fix for opera

     

    So no guarantees on when this will happen, but it's certainly not hopeless.
