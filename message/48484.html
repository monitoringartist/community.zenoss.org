    Re: zenmigrate error: Schema for relation job not found on JobStatus

    Never seen that one before. Somehow, you've got a bad JobStatus object in there. Can try rebuilding the relations in zendmd:

     

    for status in dmd.JobManager.jobs():

        status.checkRelations(repair=True)

     

    Then run zenmigrate again.

    Bingo, finally got it.

     

    Had to do:

     

    >>> for status in dmd.JobManager.jobs():
...     status.checkRelations(repair=True)
...     dmd.JobManager.jobs.manage_delObjects(status.id)

     

    Then I reindexed for good measure and commited.

     

    Thanks for pointing me in the right direction Ian.
