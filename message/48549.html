    Re: New VMware ESXi ZenPack

    I managed to get the failures down to an acceptable level.

     

    I ran the "check_esx_wbmem.py" command looped manually for 60 iterations and timed each command execution.  All requests returned OK, but a couple of the commands took over 1 minute to respond so my 30sec zCommandCommandTimeout was clearly not adequate.

     

    I increased the timeout to 150 seconds and have not experienced any further command timeouts.

     

    NOTE: I did receive _one_ General error over night and I feel this is acceptable for now.

     

    Cheers.
