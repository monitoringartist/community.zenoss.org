    88. Jul 4, 2010 11:22 PM (in response to Matt Ray)
    Re: New ZenPacks in the Community

    Thanks to Community member Nick Anderson for submitting his APC PDU Aggregate A/B Monitor ZenPack.  Originally posted on his blog (Monitoring  A/B PDU Load with Zenoss), this ZenPack aggregates the total AMP load from two APC PDUs.  Each piece of equipment has redundant power supplies and has access to  both legs of power. This allows one circuit to fail without bringing  down equipment. In order to ensure that failure of one circuit does not  cause the second circuit to overload and fail the aggregate AMP load  must be monitored.  This ZenPack allows you to monitor this.

     

    Thanks Nick!
