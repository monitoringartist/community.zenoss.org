    Re: New Bridge MIB ZenPack

    You have stooped and started Zenoss with zenoss stop | zenoss start?

     

    You will then need to discover devices into the /Network/Switch/BridgeMIB device class.  You may be able to move devices into that class but it seems safer to discover them afresh into that class.  Such devices should then have the Bridge Interfaces component.

     

    I assume you have checked the thread at docs/DOC-3583 ?
