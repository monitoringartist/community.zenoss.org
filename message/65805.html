    Re: Add OS Manufacturer and Model

    Yeah for some reason manufacturer is actually commented out in the modeler code:

    https://github.com/zenoss/Community-Zenpacks/blob/master/ZenPacks.AndreaConsadori.Microtick/ZenPacks/AndreaConsadori/Microtick/modeler/plugins/MKDeviceMap.py

     

        snmpGetMap = GetMap({

          #  '.1.2.840.10036.3.1.2.1.2.2' : 'manufacturer',

            '.1.3.6.1.4.1.14988.1.1.3.9.0' : 'setHWProductKey',

            '.1.3.6.1.4.1.14988.1.1.4.1.0' : 'setHWSerialNumber',

            '.1.3.6.1.4.1.14988.1.1.4.4.0': 'setOSProductKey',

             })

    You might get lucky enough to just uncomment that line in your file on your file system and restart, but who knows for sure
