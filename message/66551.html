Spam situation better - explanation of what we did

A few weeks back, the height of a few months worth of forum spam came to a peak. I want to update everyone on the progress made.

 

We crafted an immediate solution to the spam a few weeks ago, at the height. We enabled new account creation moderation and began manually OKing all new accounts. We received hundreds and then increased to thousands of new account attempts, and quickly became backlogged on the effort. Jive, the content platform hosters, finally stepped in and allowed us to mass-approve and deny new users, to get rid of the backlog. We spent hours of manual work to get this successful.

 

18% of registrations were legit. Over 1500 were automated spam agents, likely using a tool like http://ubotstudio.com/

 

Spam is being handled much better now, as new user registrations are way down. They are no longer trying to hammer us with new account creations. Stuff is working! In the near future, we will add in a reCaptcha form when creating new accounts. Even 4chan and Anonymous were unable to break reCaptcha.

 

Enjoy the spam-free forums, and let us know if you spot any more!
