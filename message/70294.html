Hello,

    Hey nirav

    Make sure you configured zenoss to send clear notifications.

     

    Events -> Triggers > notifications

    Double click on your notification rule, It supposed to be email notification rule.

    Make sure you marked the checkbox for "Send Clear"

    In addition you can change the "Clear" message content In the content tab
