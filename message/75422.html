    Re: How I can reduce the load average of my zenoss server?

    Hello,

     

    You can try using a Distributed Collectors setup. Having the collector(s) do all the polling/writing performance data should reduce load on the master. This setup is normally used for 1000+ devices or when you want to "separate" networks for security purposes.

      There's also the tuning solution. There are plenty of posts about that.

     

    Good luck,
