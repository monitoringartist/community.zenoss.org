Zenoss QA Test Day - 2.5.2 Beta/Beta RC

This Thursday will be the second official Zenoss QA Test Day. We will be covering the fixes that went into the Event Console (many of which were issues found during the last QA Test Day) as well as a large number of other fixes in this maintenance release.

 

In addition to the normal #zenoss IRC chat, there will also be the #zenoss-testing, that will on Thursday hold the Zenoss QA team as well as anyone else that chooses to participate.  You can also post related questions on the zenoss-testing forum and we'll follow threads there as well.

 

For beta artifacts, please check the following site:

http://alpha.zenoss.com/files/2.5.2-beta

 

Current beta artifacts include Cent4 and Cent5 RPMs (32 and 64 bit), our Stack installers (32 and 64 bit), Debian installers (32 and 64 bit), and the Community VMware appliance.

 

The test day will kick off at 10am EST and will run through 6pm EST.


For those of you that wish to join, we will be running this session on

 

Server: irc.freenode.net (port 6667)

Channel: #zenoss-testing

 

A transcript of this chat will remain on community/documentation/irc.
